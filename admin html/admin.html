Welcome Admin

Claims & Identity Verification

Admins can view and monitor item claims submitted by users. They are responsible for overseeing identity validation when users try to claim an item through the chat system. Admins can also mediate disputes between users.

Reported Users and Content

Admins can see reports made against inappropriate users or posts. They can review evidence and take necessary actions such as warnings, suspensions, or deletions.

System Activity Logs

Admins can track all claim requests, chat activities, and other user actions to ensure transparency and maintain trust within the system.

User Management

Admins have the ability to manage user accounts, including activating, deactivating, or editing account details to maintain platform integrity.
